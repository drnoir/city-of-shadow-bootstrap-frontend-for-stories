City of Shadow Chapter 1
Eyes like Burning Nebulae

Another stormy night on the streets of Nexus 4, red LED lights flickering and strobe like some endless noir carousel outside, heavy rain falling on the window of your Tesla B15 car as you speed towards the crime scene.

Your partner Max radios in:
Come in via the side entrance, Comrade Terrence, I'll meet you on the crime scene

You pull up on the side street near a techhead shop and get out, slowly walking into the worn down and battered residential building. Climbing up the stairs and opening the door into a dank room you see a body lying limply on the floor, traces of blood and stuggle marked on the walls and floor, Max stands at the entrance beckoning to you with his hand.
